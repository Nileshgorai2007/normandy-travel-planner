Essential Travel Tips

Pro tips for Indian travelers visiting Normandy

Visa & Documents

Indian passport holders need a Schengen visa. Apply at the French Embassy or VFS Global at least 3 weeks before travel. Keep hotel bookings & return tickets ready.

Language Tips

French is the primary language. Learn basic phrases: "Bonjour" (Hello), "Merci" (Thank you), "S'il vous plaît" (Please). English is spoken in tourist areas.

Electricity & SIM

France uses Type C/E plugs (220V). Carry a universal adapter. Get a prepaid SIM at Paris CDG airport or use an international roaming plan.

Getting Around

SNCF trains run from Paris to major Normandy cities. Book on SNCF Connect for best prices. Consider renting a car for countryside flexibility.

Money & Tipping

The Euro (€) is the currency. Cards accepted widely, but carry cash for small towns. Tipping is not mandatory but 5-10% is appreciated.

Safety

Normandy is very safe. Standard precautions apply — watch valuables in crowded areas. Emergency number: 112 (EU-wide). Pharmacies are marked with green crosses.
