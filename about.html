About This Site

What we built and why

This website is a multi-page virtual travel guide dedicated to the historic region of Normandy, France. It was created to explore and celebrate Normandy's rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, iconic D-Day beaches, medieval architecture, and world-famous culinary traditions — all from a digital lens.

The primary purpose of this project was to combine French language learning with practical web development skills, creating an immersive and informative experience for anyone curious about one of France's most historically significant regions.
